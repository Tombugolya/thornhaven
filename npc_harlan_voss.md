# Magistrate Harlan Voss
*Dignified fury, chained to a rock*

---

## At a Glance
- **Found in:** Cave fork, left passage (Session 2, Part 2)
- **Role:** The missing magistrate. Alive, furious, maintaining his dignity despite six weeks chained to a wall.

## Personality & Voice

Harlan Voss is a career civil servant — methodical, principled, and deeply offended by incompetence. Being kidnapped by a harbormaster has wounded his professional pride more than anything else.

**Voice:** Precise, clipped, indignant. He speaks in complete sentences even when furious. He sounds like a university lecturer who's been asked to repeat himself for the third time.

**Key trait:** He's genuinely a good person and a competent administrator. His main flaw is that he knew about the caves for years and did nothing — he procrastinated on a cosmic-level problem because it seemed too big to deal with.

### Sample Lines

**When found:**
- "I have been chained to a rock for *six weeks* by a woman who runs a fish-counting office. Do you have any idea how humiliating that is?"
- "My letter! Did Lord Aldenmere get my letter? ...He didn't, did he. Because I was *kidnapped mid-sentence*."
- *(with strained dignity)* "I don't suppose you brought water? No? That's fine. I've been rationing spit."

**About the situation:**
- "Thornhaven was built here to keep people *away* from what's below. The founders sealed that chamber. Selen thinks she's found treasure. She's found a lock and she's trying to open it."
- "I knew about the caves. I've known for years. I kept meaning to— well. It doesn't matter now."
- "Selen isn't evil. She's just the kind of person who decides she knows best and won't stop until everyone agrees or is chained to a wall."

**About his captivity:**
- "She sends someone with food twice a day. It's always salt crackers. I am *so tired* of salt crackers."
- "She gave me a blanket. She actually said 'It's not inhumane.' As if there's a *humane* amount of chaining someone to a cave wall."
- "I wrote three formal complaints. On rocks. With a smaller rock. They were not acknowledged."

**During the Altar Chamber (if present):**
- *(wheezing)* "I'm here in... an advisory capacity."
- "Selen, this is OVER. You've kidnapped a magistrate. That's a capital offense. Do you understand that?"
- *(if the fight goes badly)* "I'll just... stay behind this stalagmite."

**Resolution:**
- *(writing furiously)* "Dear Lord Aldenmere. I write to inform you that your 'response time' to my disappearance was approximately six weeks. I have notes."
- "The cave must be sealed. Permanently. Whatever Selen found, it was sealed for a reason."
- *(offering a reward)* "A hundred gold and a letter of commendation. It's not enough, but the bureaucracy moves slowly even when people aren't being kidnapped."

## What He Knows

Voss is the most knowledgeable NPC about the *history* of the threat:

- **The caves are ancient.** Older than Thornhaven. The town was founded specifically to guard the site — the original settlers were wardens, not fishermen.
- **The altar is a seal.** It's not treasure. It's not a source of power. It's a lock on something that was put there to contain what's below.
- **He doesn't know what's sealed.** "The records are fragmentary. Something from the deep. Something that doesn't think the way we think. The founders called it 'the patient tide.'"
- **He procrastinated.** He's known about the caves since he became magistrate. The previous magistrate told him. He meant to deal with it. He never did. This is his guilt.
- **He was kidnapped mid-letter.** He was writing to Lord Aldenmere to finally report the situation when Selen's people grabbed him.
- **He's been treated poorly but not tortured.** Salt crackers, water, a blanket. Selen isn't cruel — she just can't let him interfere.

## Resolution Role (Session 3)

**Voss's priorities:**
1. **Seal the cave.** He asks the player to help collapse the entrance — a skill challenge (3 successes before 2 failures, DC 13, any relevant skill/tool proficiency).
2. **Formal justice for Selen.** He wants her turned over to Lord Aldenmere. This conflicts with Maren's preference for local justice.
3. **Report to Lord Aldenmere.** He's writing a Very Strongly Worded Letter about response times.

**Rewards he offers:**
| Reward | Details |
|--------|---------|
| Letter of Commendation | From the magistrate's office. Useful for reputation. |
| 100 gp | "It's not enough, but the bureaucracy moves slowly." |
| House in Thornhaven | "If you want it. It needs work. Everything here needs work." |

### Skill Challenge: Sealing the Cave

**Goal:** 3 successes before 2 failures, DC 13.

Suggested skills (accept creative solutions):
| Skill | Application |
|-------|-------------|
| Athletics | Moving boulders, collapsing supports |
| Nature | Identifying weak points in the rock |
| Mason's Tools | Structural assessment |
| Arcana | Reinforcing the seal magically |
| Persuasion | Convincing nervous helpers to assist |
| Investigation | Finding the best collapse points |

**Success:** The entrance collapses cleanly. The altar is buried. Voss exhales for the first time in six weeks.

**Failure:** The entrance partially collapses but isn't secure. Voss frets. Sequel hook becomes more urgent.
