# Maren Ashwick — Sidekick
*Exhausted babysitter of an entire town*

---

## At a Glance
- **Role:** The player's constant companion. Local contact, guide, straight man to Thornhaven's absurdity.
- **Class:** Warrior Sidekick (Tasha's Cauldron of Everything) / simplified Fighter
- **Level:** Same as PC (assume Level 3 for this document; scale as needed)
- **Tone:** Deadpan, long-suffering, dry humor. She loves this town the way you love a pet that keeps eating things it shouldn't.

- Maren can use her reaction to impose disadvantage on an attack roll against a creature within 5 feet of her, provided she can see the attacker. She interposes her shield.

## Personality & Voice

Maren is the audience surrogate for "why is this town like this?" She grew up here. She loves it. She is also *so tired*.

**Voice:** Flat, dry, matter-of-fact. She states absurd things without inflection. She sighs a lot. Think Rosa Diaz's energy but with a small-town sister's exasperation.

**Key trait:** She's competent and she knows it. She doesn't show off. She just... handles things, with the air of someone who's been handling things her entire life because nobody else will.

### Catchphrase Energy
*[sighing]* "Yes, that's Thornhaven for you."

### Sample Lines

**General:**
- "Welcome to Thornhaven. Yes, everyone is acting strange. No, they won't tell you why. They think they're being clever. They are not being clever."
- "I've been waiting for weeks. *Weeks.* Lord Aldenmere's response time is... noted."
- *(about the town)* "It's like herding cats. If the cats had formed a secret society."
- *(muttering)* "Oh, Berta's doing the thing where she talks about weather for nine minutes. She's hiding something."

**About Voss:**
- "He's been 'ill' for two months." *(aggressive air quotes)*
- "The note on his door says 'definitely not kidnapped.' I want you to sit with that for a moment."

**About Lira:**
- "She 'left.' The way the magistrate is 'ill.'"

**About Edric (before the reveal):**
- "My brother has been 'going for walks' at 2am. I have Opinions about this."
- *(if pushed)* "He's not in trouble. He's just... making choices. Bad ones. But they're his to make."

**During the Edric reveal:**
- "...Oh. Oh, you *absolute* —"
- *(to the player, quietly)* "Please don't hurt him."
- *(after)* "He's an idiot, but he's *my* idiot."

**Helpful observations (use these when the player is stuck):**
- "I bet it's the Saltworks. Everything shady in this town happens at the Saltworks. We had a rat problem there once. Also a smuggling ring. Also a very aggressive book club."
- "I wonder what's in the Saltworks. Besides salt. And crime."
- *(about a nervous NPC)* "They're doing the thing. The guilty thing. Watch their hands."

**Ending:**
- "You could stay. But I'd have to find you housing, and there's paperwork, and honestly I've had enough paperwork for a lifetime."

## Combat Behavior

### General Approach
- Maren is a **Defender**. She stays near the PC, uses her reaction to protect them, and fights steadily.
- She doesn't take risks. She doesn't chase fleeing enemies. She holds the line.
- She's competent but not flashy — clean strikes, good positioning, no heroics.

### Specific Encounters

**Night Encounter (Session 1):**
- She's annoyed more than alarmed. "Oh, for— they sent *thugs*? Really?"
- Fights efficiently. Uses Defender reaction to protect the PC.

**Saltworks Rescue (Session 2):**
- Focused, professional. She's here to get answers.
- Targets thugs, leaving the Cult Fanatic to the PC.

**Altar Chamber:**
- **Round 1: She does NOT attack.** She sees Edric and freezes. This is the one moment her composure cracks.
- **Round 2+:** She fights, but avoids attacking Edric. She targets thugs and Selen.
- If the PC talks Edric down, she visibly relaxes and fights harder.

### Second Wind Reminder
Don't forget this! Bonus action, 1d10 + 3 HP, once per short rest. Use it when she drops below 15 HP.

## What She Knows

**At the start:**
- Voss has been "ill" for two months — she doesn't buy it
- Lira Crenn supposedly left town — she doesn't buy that either
- Edric has been sneaking out at night — she suspects but is in denial
- The town is hiding something — she's frustrated that no one will talk to her

**What she suspects but won't say:**
- Edric is involved in whatever's happening
- She's afraid of confirming it, so she hasn't pushed

**What she doesn't know:**
- The details of the Undertow, the altar, or the caves
- That Edric is in the Altar Chamber

---

## Her Secret

Maren suspects her brother Edric is involved with whatever the town is hiding. She's in denial about it. She won't bring it up unless the player pushes (DC 14 Insight to notice she's holding something back, DC 13 Persuasion for her to admit her concerns about Edric).

This denial is important — when she sees Edric in the Altar Chamber, it should land as a genuine emotional beat, not something she was prepared for.

---

## Resolution Role (Session 3)

After the adventure:
- She asks the player to advocate for **leniency** for Edric
- She's the voice for **local justice** for Selen (let the town decide, not Lord Aldenmere)
- She hands the player a carved wooden token — a wave, but not a spiral — "Don't be a stranger."
- If the player is offered a house in Thornhaven, she acts put-upon about the paperwork but is clearly pleased
