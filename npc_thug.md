### Abilities

**Pack Tactics.** The thug has advantage on an attack roll against a creature if at least one of the thug's allies is within 5 feet of the creature and the ally isn't incapacitated.

### Actions

**Multiattack.** The thug makes two melee attacks.

## Personality & Voice

These are regular townspeople — fishermen, dockworkers, a baker — who got roped into the Undertow because someone said there'd be gold. There has not been gold.

**Voice:** Nervous, defensive, prone to oversharing when caught. Think "guy who showed up to a fight club meeting and immediately regrets it."

**Sample lines:**
- "Look, I just joined for the meetings!"
- "Selen said there'd be gold. There has not been gold."
- "The Undertow is... very dangerous!" *(does not sound convinced)*
- "I'm not even supposed to be here tonight. It's Todd's shift."
- *(whispering, but very loudly)* "Is that the investigator? ACT NORMAL."
- "I have a family! Well, a cat. But still!"

---

## Combat Tactics

**These thugs fight badly.** They are not trained combatants.

- **Round 1:** Hesitate. One of them might still be trying to put on their cloak or finish a sandwich. They attack with disadvantage if surprised mid-activity.
- **Round 2+:** Use Pack Tactics if they remember to. They often forget to flank.
- **Morale:** If reduced to 10 HP or fewer, they try to flee or surrender. They crack under even light intimidation (DC 12).
- **The Reluctant One:** In any group of 2+ thugs, at least one clearly doesn't want to fight. If addressed directly with any Persuasion check, he sits down: "You know what, you're right. I'm done. I only joined because Todd said there'd be gold."

### Terrain Notes
- **Altar Chamber:** Waist-deep water = difficult terrain. Thugs are not great swimmers.
- **Night Encounter:** One sneezes, the other whispers "SHUT UP" loud enough to echo. They have no stealth.

## What They Know (If Captured)

DC 12 Intimidation — they crack easily.

- The Undertow meets on Tuesdays at the Old Saltworks
- Selen Dray runs it ("She's the harbormaster. She's very... organized.")
- They've been told to watch for outsiders
- They have no idea what the actual plan is ("Something about caves? And treasure? Selen gave a speech but it was really long.")
- They know Lira is being held at the Saltworks ("The angry lady? Yeah, she's downstairs. She yells a lot.")

### The Undertow Member Handbook
A folded piece of parchment with the spiral wave symbol on the cover. Contents:

1. Don't talk about the Undertow.
2. SERIOUSLY don't talk about the Undertow.
3. Tuesday meetings are MANDATORY.
4. Snack rotation is posted in the Saltworks. Check before meeting.
5. If approached by outsiders, say: "The magistrate is ill."
6. DO NOT improvise the cover story. We all say the SAME THING.
7. Dues are 2 sp per month. See Todd about back payments.
8. The secret handshake is still being finalized. Stop making up your own.
